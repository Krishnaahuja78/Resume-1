The Spark Behind the Brand

From a young age, I've been fascinated by the intersection of creativity and business. What began as doodles in notebooks evolved into full-fledged brand strategies that challenge conventional thinking.

At 19, while most of my peers were focused on academics, I saw an opportunity in the haircare industry - a gap between what existed and what consumers truly wanted. This insight became the foundation for Rebel Hairs.

My journey hasn't been linear. From academic excellence to NGO work, from student council leadership to building a business from scratch - each experience has shaped my perspective on what it means to create value in today's world.

Why We Started

The haircare industry was saturated with products that overpromised and underdelivered. We saw an opportunity to create something different - products that spoke honestly to consumers, with formulations that actually worked.

Rebel Hairs was born from this frustration with the status quo. We wanted to build a brand that stood for transparency, quality, and rebellious authenticity.
